Frequently Asked Questions & Answers

Q1: How do I sign up for music lessons?

A: You can sign up by completing the enquiry form on this page. Once submitted, we’ll contact you within 24–48 hours to confirm your details and schedule your first lesson.

Q2: What instruments do you teach?

A: We currently offer lessons for:

  • Theory in any instrument.
  • Guitar (acoustic and electric)
  • Piano

Q3: Are lessons available online or only in person?

A: Both options are available. You can choose:

  • Online lessons from the comfort of your home
  • In-person lessons

Q4: What age groups do you teach?

A: We teach students of all ages, including children, teenagers, and adults.

Q5: How long is each lesson?

A: Lesson durations may vary, but typically range between 30 minutes to 1 hour depending on the student’s needs.
