About us

Our Story

Matthew’s Music School was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make quality music education accessible, enjoyable, and inspiring for everyone. Based in Athlone, Cape Town, the school was created to provide students with a supportive environment where they can grow musically at their own pace.

Our Mission

To empower students through music by building confidence, creativity, and technical skill through enjoyable learning experiences.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and respected music school in Cape Town and beyond, known for nurturing talent and fostering a lifelong love for music.

Our Teaching Approach

At Matthew’s Music School, we believe that every student learns differently. That’s why we:

  • Tailor lessons to each student’s goals and ability
  • Balance practical playing with music theory
  • Encourage creativity and self-expression
  • Focus on building confidence through progress

Our goal is not just to teach music, but to help students enjoy the learning process.

Meet the Instructor

Matthew is a passionate music educator dedicated to helping students succeed. With a strong foundation in both practical performance and music theory, he creates lessons that are engaging, structured, and enjoyable.
